Our aim


  • Our aim is to build a portal which lets the victim file his or her complaint with ease.

  • Our focus is also to provide the victims with adequate resources with which they can accurately describe their problem

  • Our portal's design is minimalist, thus emphasizing on its usability.

  • We have prioritized on a clean and ordered database, security of user data and the user-friendliness and interactiviity of the website.

    • Facets of the website


    • Complaint registry:
      The user can input his or her problem in the portal's complaint section. The input will be in the form of a text, describing the problem he or she is facing and against whom.
      • This will give a more profound evidence of when, how, where and by whom the victim was bullied.
    • Ticket generation:
      A ticket will be generated for every complaint. This ticket will possess a unique ID. This will help the consultant and the user to uniquely identify the complaint and track down the progress made by the officials in solving the issue.
    • Tags:
      Complaints will be classified on the basis of tags. This allows the officials to search for problems on the basis of tags.
      • This will include a certain level of flexibility to the search process and the officials as well since they can now search for tags in their area of expertise.
    • Data analysis:
      Data will be analyzed on a regular basis and shown in the analytics dashboard.
      • Analytical data will include the location(s) and the tag(s) associated with the complaint
      This will help in identifying the areas which are infested with the heinous crime of cyberbullying
      This will also bring into our notice the types of cyberbullying occuring in those areas, including the frequency at which the complaints are addressed and solved by the authorities.
    • Messaging system:
      Email/sms will be sent with third party modules.
