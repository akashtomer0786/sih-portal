The laws we operate under give us the power to help people who are suffering from serious cyberbullying.

Before you start

Before you fill the form, please make sure that:

- You have reported the incident to any person you can confide in.

- You have enough evidence or information about the cyberbullying.

Do you have support

We understand that many people, especially kids and teens, experience different feelings as a result of cyberbullying.

If, as a minor, you feel bad, sad or stressed while you fill out the complaint form, please talk to someone you know or/and trust.

In case of any doubt, contact Kids Helpline if you are a kid and if you are a teen, you can contact the Teen Helpline
